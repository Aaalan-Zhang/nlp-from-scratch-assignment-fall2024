An incessantly ringing cell phone in a quiet café . A stranger at the next table who has had enough of it . And a dead man with a lot of loose ends . So begins Dead Man 's Cell Phone . Dead Man 's Cell Phone is a work about how we remember the dead – and how that memorialization changes us .
