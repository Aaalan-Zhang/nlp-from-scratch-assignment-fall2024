Our Mission To create a transformative educational experience for students focused on deep disciplinary knowledge ; problem-solving ; leadership , communication and interpersonal skills ; and personal health and well-being . To cultivate a transformative university community committed to ( a ) attracting and retaining diverse , world-class talent ; ( b ) creating a collaborative environment open to the free exchange of ideas , where research , creativity , innovation and entrepreneurship can flourish ; and ( c ) ensuring individuals can achieve their full potential . To impact society in a transformative way - regionally , nationally and globally - by engaging with partners outside the traditional borders of the university campus .
