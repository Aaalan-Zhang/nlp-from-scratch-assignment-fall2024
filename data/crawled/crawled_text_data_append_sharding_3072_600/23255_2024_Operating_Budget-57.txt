The Bureau of Police assigns an officer/helmsman who provides a law enforcement component to the units . This unit conducts port security and safety patrols on a routine basis . For the SWAT Team , EMS provides trained Tactical EMS ( TEMS ) personnel who have undergone all of the required SWAT training in addition to specialized treatment under fire training . They respond as an integral element of the SWAT Team to multiple incidents such as barricaded persons , high risk warrants , and dignitary protection . For the Hazmat Team , EMS participates with the Bureaus of Fire and Police , providing personnel trained and certified to the Technician level for entry , evaluation , mitigation , and decontamination . EMS also provides the required medical monitoring for pre-entry and post-entry for an incident .
