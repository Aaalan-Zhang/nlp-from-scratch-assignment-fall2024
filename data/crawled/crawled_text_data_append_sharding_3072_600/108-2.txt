While it 's widely known that the Steelers are named for Pittsburgh 's rich steel history , few recognize the meaning behind the logo . Each of the hypocycloids represents one of the three materials used to make steel . Yellow represents coal , red is for iron ore and steel scrape is illustrated with blue . Why do Steelers fans chant “ Here We Go Steelers ? ” During games , you 'll hear fans chanting `` Here We Go , Steelers ! '' While it seems like a simple chant , there 's some history behind this one too . “ Here We Go , ” originally written by Rodger Wood and recorded in 1994 , was and still is intended as a Steelers fight song .
