The Trust currently operates five distinct exhibition spaces in downtown Pittsburgh ’ s 14-block Cultural District including Wood Street Galleries , a historic exhibition space dedicated to new and emerging media . The Galleries at the Pittsburgh Cultural Trust are FREE and open to the public .
