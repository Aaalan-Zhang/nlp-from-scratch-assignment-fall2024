[ 84 ] The recognition of Carnegie Mellon as one of the best research facilities in the nation has a long history . As early as the 1987 federal budget , CMU was ranked as third in the amount of federal research funds received with $ 41.5 million , with only MIT and Johns Hopkins receiving more research funds from the Department of Defense . [ 85 ] The Pittsburgh Supercomputing Center ( PSC ) is a joint effort between Carnegie Mellon , University of Pittsburgh , and Westinghouse Electric Company . PSC was founded in 1986 by its two scientific directors , Ralph Roskies of the University of Pittsburgh and Michael Levine of Carnegie Mellon .
