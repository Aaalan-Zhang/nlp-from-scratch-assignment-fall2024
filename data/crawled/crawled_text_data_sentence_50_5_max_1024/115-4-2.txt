[ 70 ] Tennis : Historically the Pittsburgh Triangles of the 1970s played tennis at the Civic Arena , winning the WTT championship in 1975 . The arena also hosted the Pittsburgh Open tennis tournament from 1979 until 1984 . The year-round facilities at Mellon Park Tennis Center currently serve the city . Gaelic football : Teams such as the Celtics ( men 's ) and Banshees ( women 's ) , founded in 1976 and 2002 , respectively . The Celtics won the Midwest title in 2002 , 2006 , and won both the midwest and National titles in 2011 . The Banshees won the Midwest title in 2004 , 2005 , and both the midwest and Junior B shield national title in 2011 . Roller derby : Steel City Derby Demons of the WFTDA . Dodgeball : NDL 's Pittsburgh Punishers .
